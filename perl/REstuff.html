In the regular expressions from the secret program
  • b - indicates word boundary
  • i - indicates ignore case
Other valid predefined character classes are:
  • d - digits [0-9]
  • D - not digits [^0-9]
  • w - word [a-zA-Z0-9_]
  • W - not word [^a-zA-Z0-9_]
  • s - space [ \r\t\n\f]
  • S - not space [^ \r\t\n\f]
